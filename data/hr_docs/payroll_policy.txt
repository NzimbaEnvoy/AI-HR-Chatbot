Payroll and Compensation Policy

Pay Cycle
Employees are paid monthly on the last working day of the month.

Payslips
Electronic payslips are issued via the HR system.

Salary Adjustments
Salary reviews are conducted annually based on performance and company performance.

Overtime
Overtime must be approved in advance by management.
Overtime compensation follows labour regulations.

Tax and Deductions
Statutory deductions including PAYE and UIF apply.
Employees receive annual tax certificates (IRP5).

Payroll Queries
Payroll issues should be submitted via HR ticketing.
