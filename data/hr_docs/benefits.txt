Employee Benefits Policy

Medical Aid

The company provides medical aid support to eligible employees.
The company covers 60% of the medical aid contribution.
Employees may include dependants at their own cost.
Medical aid plans include hospital cover, chronic illness benefits, and preventative care.
Changes to medical aid selections must be submitted during the annual benefits window unless qualifying life events occur.

Pension Fund

The company provides a pension fund for retirement savings.
The company matches employee contributions up to 7.5% of basic salary.
Employees may increase voluntary contributions subject to payroll processing timelines.
Pension benefits become accessible according to retirement fund rules and legislation.

Employee Assistance Programme (EAP)

The company provides confidential counselling services through the Employee Assistance Programme.
Employees can access support for mental health, financial stress, family issues, or workplace challenges.
Services are confidential and do not affect employment status.

Wellness Programme

Employees have access to wellness initiatives including health screenings, mental wellness sessions, and fitness campaigns.
Wellness resources may include online training, webinars, or company-sponsored health events.

Remote Work Benefits

Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week subject to manager approval.
Remote work eligibility depends on job role and operational requirements.
Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe and secure home workspace.

Learning and Development

Employees receive access to professional development opportunities aligned with their role.
Training may include internal workshops, certifications, and external courses.
Manager approval may be required for funded training programmes.

Annual Bonus

Eligible employees may receive performance-based bonuses depending on company performance and individual performance outcomes.
Bonus eligibility criteria are communicated annually.

Leave Benefits

Employees receive annual leave, sick leave, and family responsibility leave according to company policy.
Leave benefits support employee wellbeing and work-life balance.

Insurance Benefits

Employees may be covered by group life insurance depending on employment grade.
Disability insurance may provide income protection in the event of long-term illness or injury.

Travel and Allowances

Certain roles may qualify for travel allowances or reimbursement for business-related expenses.
All expense claims must follow company policy and be approved by management.

Flexible Benefits

Employees may have access to flexible benefit options depending on company programmes.
Flexible benefits may include selecting benefit structures during annual enrolment periods.

Recognition Programmes

The company recognises outstanding performance through recognition programmes.
Recognition may include awards, bonuses, or public acknowledgement.

Eligibility

Benefits eligibility may vary depending on employment status, job level, and contractual terms.
HR can provide guidance on specific benefit eligibility.

Benefit Queries

Employees with benefit-related questions should contact HR or create an HR ticket.
