HR Leave Policy

Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 15 working days of annual leave per year.
Leave accrues monthly based on employment duration.
Annual leave must be approved by the employee’s direct manager.
Unused annual leave may carry over to the next year up to a maximum of 5 days.

Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 30 days of paid sick leave over a 3-year cycle.
A medical certificate may be required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.
Frequent sick leave patterns may be reviewed by HR.

Family Responsibility Leave
Employees are entitled to 3 days per year for family emergencies or bereavement.

Leave Requests
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR system at least 2 days in advance unless emergency leave applies.

Unpaid Leave
Unpaid leave may be granted at management discretion depending on operational requirements.

Public Holidays
Employees are entitled to all national public holidays unless operational requirements apply.

Maternity and Paternity Leave
Maternity leave follows statutory requirements.
Paternity leave is provided for 10 working days.
