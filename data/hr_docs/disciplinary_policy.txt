Code of Conduct and Disciplinary Procedures

Expected Behaviour
Employees must maintain professional conduct at all times.

Misconduct
Examples include absenteeism, insubordination, harassment, or misuse of company resources.

Disciplinary Process
Step 1: Informal counselling.
Step 2: Written warning.
Step 3: Final warning or disciplinary hearing.

Grievances
Employees may raise grievances through HR confidentially.

Appeals
Employees may appeal disciplinary outcomes within 5 working days.
