HR Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Leave and Attendance

Q: How many annual leave days do employees receive?
A: Employees receive 15 working days of annual leave per year.

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Leave must be requested via the HR system at least 2 days in advance unless it is an emergency.

Q: Can unused annual leave be carried over?
A: Yes, up to 5 unused leave days may be carried over to the next year.

Q: What happens if I take sick leave?
A: Sick leave is available up to 30 days over a 3-year cycle.

Q: When is a medical certificate required?
A: A medical certificate may be required if sick leave exceeds 2 consecutive days.

Q: Can I take unpaid leave?
A: Unpaid leave may be granted at management discretion based on operational requirements.

Q: What is family responsibility leave?
A: Employees receive 3 days per year for family emergencies or bereavement.

Q: What should I do if I need emergency leave?
A: Notify your manager immediately and submit the request in the HR system as soon as possible.

Remote Work

Q: How many remote work days are allowed?
A: Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week subject to manager approval.

Q: Do I need approval for remote work?
A: Yes, remote work requires manager approval and must align with role requirements.

Q: What are remote work expectations?
A: Employees must remain available during working hours and maintain performance standards.

Q: Can remote work be revoked?
A: Yes, remote work privileges may be adjusted based on performance or operational needs.

Benefits

Q: Does the company provide medical aid?
A: Yes, the company covers 60% of medical aid contributions.

Q: Can dependants be added to medical aid?
A: Yes, dependants may be added at the employee's own cost.

Q: Does the company contribute to pension?
A: Yes, the company matches pension contributions up to 7.5% of salary.

Q: Are wellness services available?
A: Yes, employees have access to wellness and counselling support.

Payroll

Q: When do employees get paid?
A: Employees are paid monthly on the last working day of the month.

Q: How do I access my payslip?
A: Payslips are available electronically via the HR system.

Q: What should I do if my salary is incorrect?
A: Create an HR ticket under the payroll category.

Q: Do employees receive tax certificates?
A: Yes, annual tax certificates (IRP5) are issued.

Performance and Employment

Q: Is there a probation period?
A: Yes, new employees undergo a 3-month probation period.

Q: How are performance reviews conducted?
A: Performance reviews occur annually and may include interim feedback sessions.

Q: What happens after probation?
A: Successful probation results in confirmation of permanent employment.

HR Support

Q: How do I contact HR?
A: Employees can create HR tickets through the HR system.

Q: What issues can HR help with?
A: HR assists with payroll, benefits, leave, performance concerns, and workplace issues.

Q: Are HR conversations confidential?
A: Yes, HR maintains confidentiality according to company policy.

Disciplinary and Conduct

Q: What happens if misconduct occurs?
A: HR follows a progressive disciplinary process starting with counselling and escalating as required.

Q: Can I raise a grievance?
A: Yes, grievances may be submitted confidentially to HR.

Q: Can disciplinary decisions be appealed?
A: Yes, appeals must be submitted within 5 working days.

General

Q: Where can I find HR policies?
A: HR policies are available through the company HR portal.

Q: Who approves leave?
A: Leave is approved by the employee's direct manager.

Q: Can policies change?
A: Yes, policies may be updated periodically and employees will be notified.
